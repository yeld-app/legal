Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

YELD (“the App”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

2. Data We Collect and Do Not Collect

YELD collects limited technical and usage data needed to improve app performance and reliability.

  • No photos or videos are uploaded
  • No personal profile information such as name, email address, or phone number is required to use the App
  • No advertising tracking across other apps or websites is performed

3. Analytics

The App collects limited usage analytics to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its functionality and user experience.

This data may include events such as app launches, feature usage, and interaction flows. This data may include technical identifiers and is used only for product improvement, not advertising.

We use PostHog as an analytics processor. Data is processed according to PostHog’s privacy terms and infrastructure settings.

The App does not use IDFA, does not perform cross-app tracking, and does not use analytics data for advertising or marketing purposes.

For more information, see PostHog’s Privacy Policy:
https://posthog.com/privacy

4. Crash Reporting

The App uses Sentry as a crash reporting service to monitor stability and diagnose technical issues.

Crash reports may contain technical diagnostics such as device model, operating system version, stack traces, and error context. This information is used solely to improve app stability and functionality.

Crash data is not used for advertising, marketing, or tracking across apps or websites.

For more information, see Sentry’s Privacy Policy:
https://sentry.io/privacy/

5. Photo Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to review and manage your photos.

All photo processing happens entirely on your device. Photos never leave your device.

6. Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information.

Subscription status (active/expired) may be processed in-app to provide Premium features.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
