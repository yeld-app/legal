Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

YELD (“the App”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

YELD does not collect, store, or process personal data that can identify you.

  • No photos or videos are uploaded
  • No personal information such as name, email address, or phone number is collected
  • No advertising or tracking across other apps or websites is performed

3. Analytics

The App collects anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its functionality and user experience.

This data may include events such as app launches, feature usage, and interaction flows. The data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

We use PostHog as an analytics service provider to process this anonymous usage data. PostHog processes this data on servers located in the European Union.

The App does not use IDFA, does not perform cross-app tracking, and does not use analytics data for advertising or marketing purposes.

For more information, see PostHog’s Privacy Policy:
https://posthog.com/privacy

4. Crash Reporting

The App uses Sentry as a crash reporting service to monitor stability and diagnose technical issues.

Crash reports may include technical information such as device model, operating system version, stack traces, and non-personal diagnostic data. This information does not identify individual users and is used solely to improve app stability and functionality.

Crash data is not used for advertising, marketing, or tracking across apps or websites.

For more information, see Sentry’s Privacy Policy:
https://sentry.io/privacy/

5. Photo Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to review and manage your photos.

All photo processing happens entirely on your device. Photos never leave your device.

6. Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
